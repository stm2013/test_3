Welcome

This project is a part of the course Project Engineering at the Nanoscale held at the faculty of engineering at Lund University. In this website you will find an in depth procedure in building a scanning tunneling microscope (STM) along with the respective difficulties we experienced during the implementation. We will take you through our journey from having a highly limited knowledge in the field to actually having built a fully functioning microscope capable of achieving micrometer resolution.
